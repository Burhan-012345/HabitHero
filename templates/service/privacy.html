Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

At HabitHero, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our habit-tracking application.

TL;DR: We only collect data necessary to provide our service, we don't sell your data, and we take security seriously.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile information you choose to provide

Habit Data

We collect data about your habits and progress, including:

  • Habit names and descriptions
  • Completion dates and times
  • Streak information
  • Notes you add to habit completions

Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you use HabitHero:

  • Device information
  • App usage patterns
  • Feature interactions
  • Error reports

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: To operate and maintain HabitHero features
  • Improve Experience: To understand how users interact with our app
  • Communication: To send important updates and notifications
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access
  • Analytics: To improve our services (anonymous data only)

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With your consent for specific purposes
  • With service providers who help us operate (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication methods
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@habithero.com

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you signed in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Improve performance

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

HabitHero is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
